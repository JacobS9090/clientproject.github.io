Do you have an interest in learning how to fish like a pro? If so, come on down to Coral Beach Fishing Academy. With locations all across the Eastern Seaboard, we are open to teach you how to fish and possibly acquire a fishing license. The company was founded in 1970, when three brothers visiting relatives in Corpus Christi, Texas could not find a place to settle down and fish. Those three brothers now own locations as far west as Hawaii, and have even ventured into foreign markets in Mexico.
